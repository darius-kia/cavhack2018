How To Play

The objective of this game is to collect as many basketballs as you can. As you progress, the game will be more difficult and the speed of the balls will increase. If you miss five balls, the game will be over and you can restart. To control the basketball hoop, simply move your mouse around and the basketballs will automatically collect.

About

We made this game and website as our project for the inaugral CavHack 2018. We all had lots of fun making this project and thought that you guys should have the fun in playing it!
