Boolin'

An online interactive basketball game.

How To Play

The objective of this game is to collect as many basketballs as you can using a hoop which only moves on a horizontal-axis. The hoop can be controlled by moving your mouse left to right, and the number of basketballs that are scored and missed are recorded at the top right of your screen. If you miss 5 balls, then the game is over.

About

This game was made for the inaugural hackathon of CavHack 2018. We decided to make a Java applet with our knowledge of web development to complement the program, and we thought that others would enjoy playing it just as much as we enjoyed creating it!
